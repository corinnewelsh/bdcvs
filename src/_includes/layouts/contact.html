Opening Hours

The Ripple Centre reception is open Monday to Friday 9am to 5pm.

Phone: 020 8532 7300

Email: reception@bdcvs.org.uk

Address: Ripple Centre, 121–125 Ripple Road, Barking, Essex IG11 7FN

How to find us

The nearest station is Barking, which is served by London Underground (District and Hammersmith & City lines), London Overground (trains to/from Gospel Oak) and National Rail (c2c trains to/from London Fenchurch Street).

We are less than 500 metres from Barking Station. From the station turn right. When you get to the crossroads, turn left onto Ripple Road.
